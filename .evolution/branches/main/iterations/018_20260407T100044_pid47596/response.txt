- **Modernized type hints** — Replaced `Dict`, `List`, `Optional`, `Tuple` from `typing` with builtin `dict`, `list`, `tuple`, `X | None` (already guarded by `from __future__ import annotations`). Removed unused typing imports, keeping only `Any` and `Callable`.
