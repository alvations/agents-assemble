# Sector Rotation & Thematic Research -- April 2026

## 1. Best Performing Sectors YTD 2026

The market is rotating hard away from tech into "real economy" sectors. Energy leads (+22% YTD), followed by Industrials (+16%) and Consumer Defensives (+13.3%). Technology has drifted into the "Lagging" quadrant on relative rotation graphs.

**Tickers:** XLE (energy), XLI (industrials), XLP (staples). Individual: EPD, ET, WMB (midstream pipelines, 6-8% yields, benefiting from AI/data center power demand). Less obvious: HESM (Hess Midstream, ~7.9% yield, never cut distribution), WES (Western Midstream), MPLX.

## 2. Cycle Position: Late Expansion (Stage 4)

Models place us in Stage 4 -- favorable for stocks and commodities, unfavorable for bonds. Energy (XLE) and Materials (XLB) are in the "Leading" quadrant. Consumer Staples (XLP) is rotating toward leadership as a defensive hedge. Playbook: overweight energy, materials, staples; underweight tech, discretionary.

## 4. Emerging Market Small Caps

**Vietnam (VNM):** FTSE upgrade to Emerging Market status Sept 2026. GDP +8.2%, stock market +57.7% in 2025. KRX trading system live. Foreign ownership caps removed. Massive catalyst ahead as index funds must buy.

**India (INDA):** Credit growth in double digits. Lower volatility (2.98% vs VNM 9.92%). Also: SMIN (iShares MSCI India Small-Cap).

**Indonesia (EIDO):** +8.3% trailing year. Cheap exposure to ASEAN commodity exporter.

**Broader EM:** EM equities +33% in 2025 vs ~15% S&P 500. Look at: VNM, INDA, EIDO, SMIN for country-specific plays.
