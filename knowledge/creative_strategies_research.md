# Creative / Unusual Strategy Research (2025)

## Key Findings

### 1. Retail Contrarian Outperformance
JPMorgan data: retail traders' single-stock portfolios achieved stronger
profit-to-loss ratios than institutional AI/software portfolios.
Retail's advantage: buying dips when institutions are panic selling.

### 2. "TACO" Trade (Trump Always Chickens Out)
Retail investors bought $3B in equities on April 3, 2025 during 5% drop.
Doubled down next day (another 6% drop). One week later: 9.5% surge.
**Lesson:** Political uncertainty events tend to reverse quickly.

### 3. Sector Divergence Pairs
Hedge funds bearish on: financials, industrials, energy.
Retail bullish on: tech, consumer discretionary.
**Opportunity:** Trade the spread between these groups.

### 4. High Dividend Yield Trap Contrarian
Buy stocks market dismisses as "yield traps" — the ones with
unusually high dividend yields that the market thinks will cut.
Often the market overreacts and dividend is maintained → capital appreciation.

- Satellite imagery for parking lot traffic → retail sales estimates
- Oil tanker tracking → supply predictions
- Social media sentiment → early trend detection
