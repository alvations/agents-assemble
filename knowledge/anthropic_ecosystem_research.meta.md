## Limitations

1. **Private company opacity** - Anthropic does not publicly disclose detailed financials, customer lists, or infrastructure spend
2. **Fund holding lag** - ETF/fund allocations may be 30-90 days stale depending on reporting schedule
3. **Revenue estimates** - $30B ARR is annualized from a point-in-time; actual annual revenue may differ
4. **Valuation subjectivity** - $380B post-money is a negotiated VC round price, not a market-clearing price
5. **Competitive data** - Market share figures come from third-party estimates with varying methodologies
6. **Future projections** - IPO timeline, chip development plans, and growth trajectories are subject to change
