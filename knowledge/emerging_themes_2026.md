# Emerging Technology Investment Themes (2026)

## 1. Robotics & Autonomous Vehicles
- Humanoid robots commercially viable (CES 2026)
- Level 4 autonomy: Uber robotaxis in 2026
- Global robotics market > $200B by decade end
- Physical AI: robots + AI in manufacturing, logistics, healthcare
- **Key stocks:** ISRG (surgical robots), RWLK, IROBOT (if still public)
- **ETFs:** ROBO, BOTZ, ARKQ

## 2. Space Economy
- $630B (2023) → $1.79T (2035), nearly 3x
- SpaceX IPO potential in 2026 could re-rate entire sector
- Key themes: satellite tech, space infra, orbital compute, defense
- **Key stocks:** RKLB, ASTS, LUNR, BA, LMT, NOC, PLTR
- **ETFs:** ARKX, UFO/ROKT

## 3. GLP-1 / Weight Loss Drugs
- $73-87B market in 2026 (massive acceleration)
- LLY past $1T market cap (FDA approved oral GLP-1 pill)
- 30M Americans on GLP-1 by 2030 (10M increase from 2026)
- Oral drugs coming → accessibility explosion
- **Key stocks:** LLY, NVO (Novo Nordisk), AMGN, VKTX, ALT
- **Losers from GLP-1:** MDGL, DM (diabetes legacy), food companies?

## Strategies to Consider
- Robotics + Autonomous theme strategy
- GLP-1 / Obesity theme strategy
- Space economy (already have space category)
- Combined "Future Tech" strategy: AI + Robotics + Space + GLP-1
