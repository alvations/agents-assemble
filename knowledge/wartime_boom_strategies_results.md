## Key Insights
1. **Policy > Geopolitics**: Policy announcements (infrastructure, tariffs) are more tradeable than wars/crises. policy_catalyst (+605%) crushes wartime_portfolio (+20%).
2. **Calendar effects still work**: January barometer and presidential cycle are 100+ year old patterns that still hold. Don't dismiss them.
3. **AI boom parallels are mixed**: Infrastructure layer works (like railroads in 1850s), but "adopters beat builders" doesn't hold yet. The boom is still in infrastructure phase.
4. **Bubble detection works**: late_cycle_bubble_hedge correctly rotates to value when growth gets overheated. 89% consistency = reliable.
5. **War premiums fade fast**: wartime_portfolio only 32% consistency. Markets price in war quickly, then the premium dissipates.
