### 2. Nasdaq 11-day winning streak

**Report says:** Nasdaq surged 1.59% to 24,016 -- its 11th consecutive winning session, the longest streak since 2023.

**Verdict: PARTIALLY INCORRECT**

The 11-day winning streak count is confirmed by multiple sources (Blockonomi: "extended its consecutive daily gains to 11 sessions"; CBS News: "S&P 500, Nasdaq hit record highs"). However, the comparison year is wrong. Sources consistently state this was the longest streak since **November 2021**, not 2023. (Blockonomi: "a streak not witnessed since November 2021"; MarketWatch/Facebook: "Nasdaq logs longest winning streak since 2021"; Kiplinger used "since 2023" in its headline, but this appears to be an outlier -- the dominant sourcing says 2021.)

Note: Some sources describe the streak as 10 days on April 14 (before the April 15 session extended it to 11). This is consistent -- as of the April 15 close, it was 11 consecutive winning sessions.

**Correction needed:** "the longest streak since 2023" should be "the longest streak since November 2021."

Sources: [Blockonomi](https://blockonomi.com/sp-500-breaks-historic-7000-mark-as-nasdaq-extends-win-streak-on-ceasefire-optimism/), [CBS News](https://www.cbsnews.com/news/stocks-market-today-s-p-500-record-highs/), [MarketWatch via Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/marketwatch/posts/sp-500-closes-just-shy-of-record-nasdaq-logs-longest-winning-streak-since-2021/1322692336397751/)

### 3. TSMC earnings: net income $18.1B, gross margin 66.2%, AI >55% of revenue

**Report says:** Net income NT$572.5B ($18.1B, +58% YoY), gross margin 66.2%, revenue $35.9B (+35%), AI accelerators >55% of revenue.

**Verdict: MOSTLY VERIFIED, with nuances**

- **Net income NT$572.5B (~$18.1B):** VERIFIED. Multiple sources confirm NT$572.48B net income, ~$18.1B USD, +58.3% YoY.
- **Gross margin 66.2%:** VERIFIED. Confirmed by TSMC earnings transcript, Investing.com, and multiple outlets. Significantly above guided range of 63-65%.
- **Revenue $35.9B:** VERIFIED. Confirmed across sources.
- **Revenue growth +35%:** PARTIALLY CORRECT. In New Taiwan Dollar terms, revenue grew 35.1% YoY. In US Dollar terms, it grew 40.6% YoY. The report uses "+35%" which corresponds to the TWD figure. The USD growth rate is actually higher at ~41%.
- **AI >55% of revenue:** IMPRECISE. TSMC reported HPC (High Performance Computing) at 61% of Q1 2026 revenue. However, HPC is broader than just AI -- it includes data center CPUs and other computing. TSMC's CEO CC Wei described AI demand as "extremely robust" and has guided AI accelerator revenue at a "mid- to high-50% CAGR" growth rate, but the specific claim "AI >55% of revenue" conflates the HPC segment (61%) with AI-only revenue. TSMC did not break out a separate AI-only percentage in the earnings call.

**Correction needed:** The "+35%" revenue figure is technically correct (TWD basis) but should be clarified. The "AI >55% of revenue" claim should be restated as "HPC >60% of revenue, with AI demand described as 'extremely robust.'"

Sources: [Investing.com transcript](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-tsmcs-q1-2026-shows-strong-growth-and-margin-gains-93CH-4617167), [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/16/tsmc-q1-profit-58-percent-ai-chip-demand-record.html), [SemiWiki](https://semiwiki.com/forum/threads/tsmc-q1-2026-revenue-increased-40-6-year-over-year.24948/)
