## Dividend Kings (50+ years)
- 57 companies
- Don't need to be S&P 500

## Key Insight
Dividend growth stocks have NOT kept up with tech-heavy S&P 500 recently.
They provide: lower volatility, income, defensive characteristics.
Best as a portfolio COMPONENT, not sole strategy.
