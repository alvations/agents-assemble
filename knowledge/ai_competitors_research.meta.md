## Key Findings Summary

1. **Anthropic passed OpenAI** in annualized revenue ($30B vs $24B ARR) and enterprise market share (40% vs 27%) -- the most surprising finding
2. **NVIDIA still dominates** but custom silicon (Broadcom, Marvell) is the fastest-growing segment
3. **$650B+ in AI capex** planned by Big Tech in 2026 -- the largest infrastructure buildout in history
4. **Inference overtaking training** as primary compute workload creates new winners (AMD, Broadcom)
5. **HBM memory** is sold out through 2026 (Micron, Samsung, SK Hynix) -- a critical bottleneck
6. **Power/cooling** is the most underappreciated theme -- 30x power growth projected
7. **China AI** trades at 50-80% discount to US peers despite competitive models
8. **Anthropic and Databricks IPOs** in H2 2026 will be among the largest tech IPOs ever
