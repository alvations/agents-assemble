## 3. Proven Trading Patterns (Backtested)

- **RSI(2) < 10 + 200-day MA filter:** Buy oversold in uptrend. Profitable since the 1990s across backtests.
- **3 consecutive down days -> buy, exit first up day:** Simple mean-reversion with persistent edge.
- **Triple Bottom pattern:** 87% success rate, average 45% price increase per Bulkowski's research.
- **Head & Shoulders:** 89% success rate for trend reversal identification.
- **Weekend Trend Trader system:** 22.9% CAGR backtested from 1990-present (58% max drawdown).

**Warning:** Only 1.1% of day traders earn above minimum wage (Brazilian SEC study of 1,551 traders). Swing/position trading with backtested systems vastly outperforms discretionary day trading.
