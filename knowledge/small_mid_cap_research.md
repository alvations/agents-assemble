## 3. Small Cap Value Factor Strategy

Small caps at cheapest relative valuation vs large caps in 50 years. Academic research (Fama-French) supports size + value + profitability factor combination. Multi-factor (value + quality + momentum) outperforms single-factor historically. Shift from speculative to quality small caps underway.

**Factor ETFs to backtest (ranked by factor loading):**
- `AVUV` (Avantis US Small Cap Value) -- strongest factor tilt, 0.25% ER, 13.23% annualized since Oct 2019
- `DFSV` (Dimensional US Small Cap Value) -- purest SCV exposure, 0.31% ER
- `DFAT` (Dimensional US Targeted Value) -- 0.28% ER, +25.58% trailing 1yr
- `VBR` (Vanguard Small-Cap Value) -- cheapest at 0.07% ER, 842 holdings, best for taxable
- `IJS` (iShares S&P 600 Value) -- profitability-screened index, 0.18% ER
- `SLYV` (SPDR S&P 600 Small Cap Value) -- similar to IJS
- `IWN` (iShares Russell 2000 Value)

## 4. Micro Cap / Momentum Strategy

Screening criteria: ROCE >15%, D/E <1, revenue growth >15% CAGR (3yr), positive operating cash flow 2+ years, insider holding >45%. Use 3/6/9-month momentum with 12-month return volatility filter. Extremely volatile -- position sizing critical.

**Micro-cap ETFs to backtest:**
- `IWC` (iShares Micro-Cap) -- Russell Microcap Index, returned 22.45% in 2025, $160/share
- `FDM` (First Trust Dow Jones Select MicroCap) -- 0.60% ER, +21.44% trailing 1yr
