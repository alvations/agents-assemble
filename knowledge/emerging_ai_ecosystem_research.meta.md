## Key Data Points Confirmed

### HuggingFace
- $400M total funding across 8 rounds
- $4.5B valuation (Series D, Aug 2023)
- $130.1M revenue in 2024 (up from $70M in 2023)
- 34+ investors including 8 publicly traded tech giants
- No IPO filed as of April 2026
- LeRobot robotics initiative with NVIDIA partnership
- AWS and Google Cloud as primary cloud partners

### Thinking Machines Lab
- $2B seed round (largest in history) at $12B valuation (Jul 2025)
- Founded Feb 2025 by Mira Murati (ex-OpenAI CTO)
- 22 investors including NVIDIA, AMD, Cisco, ServiceNow, a16z
- 1GW NVIDIA Vera Rubin compute deal (Mar 2026)
- Tinker product launched for LLM fine-tuning
- Reportedly seeking $50B valuation (Nov 2025 talks)
- DISTINCT from Thinking Machines Data Science (Philippines, $8.1M Series A)

### Unsloth AI
- Only $500K raised (YC seed, Oct 2024)
- Founded 2023 by Daniel & Michael Han
- 2M+ monthly downloads, 500+ model support
- Collaborates with Google, OpenAI, Meta, Mistral, NVIDIA
- 2x faster training, 70% less VRAM
- Optimized for NVIDIA Blackwell (NVFP4 precision)
- No Series A announced as of April 2026

### Reka AI
- $168M total funding across 2 rounds
- $1B valuation (Series B, Jul 2025) -- unicorn status
- NVIDIA and Snowflake co-led Series B
- Oracle OCI for GPU training infrastructure
- 60+ enterprise deployments
- Founded by ex-DeepMind/Google Brain team
- Multimodal (text, image, audio, video)
