## 1. Executive Summary

OpenAI is the most valuable private company in history at $852 billion and the most anticipated tech IPO since Facebook. Having converted to a Public Benefit Corporation (PBC) in October 2025, the company is laying the groundwork for a listing as early as Q4 2026. Revenue has scaled from $2B (2023) to $6B (2024) to $20B (2025) to a $24B run rate in April 2026, with enterprise now accounting for 40% of revenue and growing toward parity with consumer by year-end.

**Key investment thesis:** OpenAI commands the largest AI ecosystem in history with 92% of Fortune 500 as customers, 1 million+ business customers, and deep strategic partnerships with Microsoft (27% stake + Azure exclusivity through 2032), Amazon ($50B + AWS), NVIDIA ($30B equity + 10 GW GPU deployment), and SoftBank ($64.6B). The $500B Stargate project is the largest AI infrastructure buildout ever attempted. Before the IPO, investors can gain exposure through multiple public vehicles.

**Revenue trajectory:**
- 2023: $2B
- 2024: $6B
- 2025: $20B (confirmed by CFO Sarah Friar)
- Feb 2026: $25B annualized

**Enterprise adoption:** 92% of Fortune 500 use ChatGPT. 1 million+ business customers. 7 million+ ChatGPT for Work seats (up 40% in 2 months). ChatGPT Enterprise weekly messages up 8x year-over-year. Average worker saves 40-60 minutes per day.

## 11. Strategy Thesis

### 11.1 Pre-IPO Portfolio Construction

**Core position (40%):** MSFT -- most direct, safest proxy. Own a business generating its own earnings while "free-riding" the $228B OpenAI stake.

**Growth/leverage position (20%):** SFTBY + AMZN -- high-beta OpenAI proxies with additional business value. SoftBank is a pure-play AI bet; Amazon adds AWS compute revenue.

**Supply chain position (25%):** NVDA, AVGO, TSM, CRWV, ANET -- picks and shovels. These companies make money regardless of which AI company wins. Broadcom particularly compelling as custom silicon kingmaker.

**Infrastructure position (10%):** CEG, VRT, PWR -- power and cooling are the #1 bottleneck for AI scaling. Nuclear renaissance is real and multi-decade.

**Pre-IPO vehicle (5%):** ARKK or ARKVX for direct fractional OpenAI equity exposure before the IPO.

### 11.2 Key Risks

1. **Valuation risk:** $852B pre-IPO for a company burning $17B/year with 33% gross margins. Revenue must scale dramatically for the math to work.
2. **Competition:** Anthropic reportedly surpassed OpenAI revenue in April 2026. Google Gemini captured Apple Siri integration. Meta open-sourcing models.
3. **Custom silicon timeline:** Titan chip may face TSMC capacity constraints (Google, NVIDIA, AMD compete for same fab capacity).
4. **Stargate execution:** Abilene expansion already canceled. $500B is aspirational, not committed.
5. **Cash burn:** Not cash-flow positive until ~2030. Requires continued massive capital raises.
6. **IPO uncertainty:** CFO guides 2027. If delayed, pre-IPO vehicles may underperform.
7. **Apple loss:** Apple shifted to Google Gemini for Siri in January 2026 -- loss of 2 billion+ device distribution.
8. **Regulatory:** AI regulation could constrain growth or increase compliance costs.
9. **Sam Altman equity:** CEO owns zero shares. Unusual governance risk for a company this size.
