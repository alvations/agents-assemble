# LEADERBOARD

**173 strategies** ranked by **Composite Score** across 6 rolling windows (2020-2025).

## Ranking Formula



| Factor | What It Measures | Range |
|--------|-----------------|-------|
| **Avg Return** | Average total return across all 6 test windows | -100% to +∞ |
| **Consistency** | % of windows where the strategy made risk-adjusted profit (Sharpe > 0) | 0% to 100% |
| **1 - Avg \|Max DD\|** | Penalty for drawdowns — bigger crashes = lower score | 0 to 1 |

This rewards strategies that **work in multiple time periods** (not just one lucky year),
**make reasonable returns** (not just break-even), and **don't blow up** (low drawdowns).

**Test windows:** 1Y (2023, 2024, 2025), 3Y (2022-2024, 2023-2025), 5Y (2020-2024)
